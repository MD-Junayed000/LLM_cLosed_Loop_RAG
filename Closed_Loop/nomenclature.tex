\noindent \textbf{Symbols}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\begin{tabular}{@{}l p{11cm}@{}}
$L$                         & Total number of decoder layers \\
$\ell^*$                    & Selected hook layer ($\approx L/2$) \\
$\mathbf{h}_\ell$           & Hidden state at layer $\ell$ (post-attention) \\
$\mathbf{h}_{\text{out}}^{(\ell)}$ & Block output at layer $\ell$ (CEV source) \\
$\mathbf{z}_{\text{CEV}}^{(\ell)}$ & Contextualized Embedding Vector at layer $\ell$ \\
$\mathbf{z}_{\text{IAV}}^{(\ell)}$ & Intermediate Activation Vector at layer $\ell$ \\
$\boldsymbol{\phi}(\mathbf{x})$ & CEV feature vector (single mid-depth layer) \\
$\boldsymbol{\psi}(\mathbf{x})$ & IAV feature vector (single mid-depth layer) \\
$p_{\text{CEV}}$            & CEV probe hallucination probability \\
$p_{\text{IAV}}$            & IAV probe hallucination probability \\
$p_{\text{fused}}$          & Fused hallucination-risk probability \\
$w$                         & Fusion weight for CEV branch \\
$T$                         & Temperature scaling parameter \\
$\theta_{\text{hallu}}$     & Intervention threshold \\
$\theta_{\text{abs}}$       & Abstention threshold \\
$\theta_{\text{ret}}$       & Retrieval-quality threshold \\
$q_{\text{ret}}$            & Retrieval-quality score \\
$W_{\text{gate}}$, $W_{\text{up}}$, $W_{\text{down}}$ & SwiGLU FFN weight matrices \\
$\odot$                     & Element-wise (Hadamard) product \\
\end{tabular}

\noindent \textbf{Glossary of Terms}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\begin{tabular}{@{}l p{10cm}@{}}
Hallucination       & Generation of factually unsupported or source-contradictory content \\
Closed-loop control & Feedback-based system where detection outputs influence system actions \\
Forward hook        & PyTorch callback that captures intermediate tensors without modifying computation \\
Residual stream     & The additive pathway through transformer decoder blocks \\
Polarity inversion  & A probe that separates classes strongly but in the opposite label direction under distribution shift \\
Probe calibration   & Adjusting probe outputs so that predicted probabilities match empirical frequencies \\
\end{tabular}
