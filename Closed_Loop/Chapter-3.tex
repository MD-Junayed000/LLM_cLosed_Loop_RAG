\chapter{MATERIALS AND METHODOLOGY}
\label{chapter3}

This chapter outlines the research design and methodology adopted in this study. It describes the materials, procedures, and techniques used to achieve the research objectives. The purpose of this chapter is to provide sufficient detail so that the study can be replicated and evaluated for validity and reliability.

This chapter is organized as follows: Section 3.1 describes the materials used in the study. Section 3.2 presents the methodology and research design. Section 3.3 discusses the participants or data sources. Section 3.4 outlines the instruments used. Section 3.5 describes the procedure and timeline. Section 3.6 explains the data analysis techniques, and Section 3.7 highlights ethical considerations and limitations.
